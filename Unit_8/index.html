New Blog...Who Dis?

I’m a 31 year old dude who moved to a new neighborhood, still in the beginnings of a major career switch and about to have his first kid. There is a lot that I know, and plenty more that I don’t. In this blog, which started as a side project for work, I’ll chronicle my experiences, while trying to look my best. Complete with some recipes and tunes I am listening to, Hashtag Dadlife picks up at the three months prior to the due date of our first born and follows me as I prepare my war against the phrase…”I’m becoming my father.” If you’ve made it this far...welcome.
